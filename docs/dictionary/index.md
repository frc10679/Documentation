# Team Dictionary

Welcome to the Wirewolves internal glossary. Terms defined here will automatically appear in search results.

---

### A
**Acronyms**
:   A shorthand way of referring to long names (e.g., FRC, BOM).

**Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC)**
:   A system that converts an analog signal, such as a voltage, into a digital form.

### B
**BOM**
:   Bill of Materials. A comprehensive list of raw materials, assemblies, and parts needed to manufacture the robot.

**Bumpers**
:   The protective pool-noodle-based structures required by FRC rules to protect the robot.
